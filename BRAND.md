# Let's Batch — Brand & Voice Rules

A short, evolving document. Every change to the site must respect these rules.

## Voice rules

### 1. No em-dashes anywhere on the site

The voice should sound spoken, not edited. Never use `—` (em-dash) in user-facing copy.

Use periods, commas, or "and" instead.

| Avoid | Prefer |
|---|---|
| Pick your seat — solo, pair, or table of four. | Pick your seat. Solo, pair, or table of four. |
| €10 off — first 4 bookings · ends Sun 12 May | €10 off. First 4 bookings only. Ends Sun 12 May. |
| PS — this isn't a cooking class. | PS. This isn't a cooking class. |

This rule applies to all user-facing copy: headlines, body, captions, CTAs, alt text, page title, meta tags. CSS and JS comments are exempt.

## Capitalisation rules

### 2. "Batch" is capitalised when it refers to the event

When "Batch" refers to the brand, a specific edition, or the event itself, it's a proper noun and gets a capital B.

| Context | Casing | Example |
|---|---|---|
| The brand / event name | Capital B | "Let's Batch", "the next Batch", "Batch 01", "Batch 02" |
| The verb (to batch cook) | Lowercase | "batch cooking together", "we batch cook once a month" |
| The noun for a quantity | Lowercase | "a batch of cookies" (rare on this site) |

Past Batch references should be specific (e.g. "Batch 01") rather than generic ("the last batch").

## Pricing model (canonical, do not change without founder approval)

All prices are **per seat**, not per group total. Six meals per person per Batch.

| Tier | Early-bird per seat | Full price per seat |
|---|---|---|
| Solo | €75 | €85 |
| Pair | €67 | €80 |
| Table of 4 | €59 | €75 |

- Early-bird deadline: typically two weeks before the Batch.
- For Batch 02: early-bird ends Sunday 12 May 2026; Batch is Tuesday 26 May 2026.
- "Cost per meal" line on cards is a derived display value: price ÷ 6 meals, rounded to the nearest €0.50.
- Group totals shown on Pair / Table cards as a small subtext (e.g. "€134 for two", "€236 for four"). Always confirm: total = per-seat price × number of seats.

If the founder wants to change pricing, update this table first and apply the rest of the changes downstream.
